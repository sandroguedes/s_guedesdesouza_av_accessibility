A/V Accessibility

This is a project that aims to test out a few ideas in the realm of accessibility, particularly how many solutions I'm able to implement to the forms of media below within it's deadline.

To that end, I have decided to try to at least get subtitles, closed captions and transcriptions to be toggleable by the user.

MEDIA

Each piece of media below has a few options enabled for accessibility purposes. All of them have a transcript that can be toggled on and off by clicking on the "power button" below the player, while the video tracks have captions available in both english and brazilian portuguese that can be accessed from the player on under the menu on the right. Enjoy =)
